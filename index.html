Roban Hultman Kramer

Data Scientist at High Table and Gerson Lehrman Group (since 2012)

At High Table I create predictive models using machine learning and probabilistic inference techniques (SVM, NMF, regularized logistic regression, LDA topic modeling, etc.) to drive recommendations on the High Table website, as well as for internal decision-making tools at GLG.

I build visualization tools using D3 to monitor model performance and data quality, and create information-dense visualizations of social networks, Bayesian parameter spaces, and other complicated data.

Research

In my astrophysics research, I focused on the interactions between the first generations of galaxies in the Universe and the intergalactic medium (IGM) --- the gas that fills the space between galaxies. I studied the state of the IGM during the first gigayear in the history of the Universe (which is now 13.7 13.82 gigayears old).

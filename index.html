Research

In my astrophysics research, I focused on the interactions between the first generations of galaxies in the Universe and the intergalactic medium (IGM) --- the gas that fills the space between galaxies. I studied the state of the IGM during the first gigayear in the history of the Universe (which is now 13.8 gigayears old).
